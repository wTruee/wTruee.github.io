Frequently Asked Questions

How can I book a facility?▼
Book online by clicking "Book a Facility," or call (555) 123-4567 or email us. Bookings are confirmed within 24 hours.
What are the membership options?▼
Football ($45/mo), Basketball ($50/mo), Fitness ($40/mo), All-Access Bundle ($99/mo). Each includes unlimited access, weekly sessions, and member events. Annual plans save up to 20%.
Are there certified trainers available?▼
Yes! Coach Mike (Football – Tue & Thu), Coach Lisa (Basketball – Wed), Trainer Dave (Fitness Bootcamp – Mon & Sat).
Is there parking available?▼
Free on-site parking for all visitors and members, plus showers, lockers, and free WiFi throughout the arena.
Can I cancel my membership anytime?▼
Yes — cancel with 30 days notice. We also offer a pause option for up to 3 months per year. No penalty fees!
Do you offer group or corporate packages?▼
Absolutely! Custom packages for families, schools, sports clubs, and corporations. Contact us for a tailored quote.

Our Story

From a Dream to an Arena

ORION Sports Arena was founded in 2018 by a group of lifelong athletes who shared one frustration — their city had no world-class venue that brought every sport under one roof.

They pooled resources, secured a location, and spent 18 months building a facility they'd always wished existed. The name ORION was chosen deliberately — the hunter constellation, always reaching, always striving higher.

What started with a single football pitch and six members has grown into a full multi-sport complex hosting over 600 active members, weekly leagues, tournaments, and AI-powered training tools used daily across all sports.
